Dear Parents/Guardian/Student,

As part of our commitment to ensuring the health and safety of our students and employees, we kindly request your assistance in completing the Medical Information Form enclosed with this letter.

The form aims to gather medical details about you/your child, enabling us to better support your/their well-being in school and in various activities including field trips, sports, and other school-related events. It covers information such as existing medical conditions, allergies, medications, and emergency contact details. Rest assured that all information provided will be treated with the utmost confidentiality and will only be accessible to authorized personnel.

Your prompt completion of this form is crucial in helping us provide the best care for you/your child. Kindly return the completed form to us at your earliest convenience.

Thank you for your cooperation in this matter.
